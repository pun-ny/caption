근근이 먹고 살아가는 현실을 적나라하게 보여주는 영상이다.
The Korean idiom "ipp-e pulchilhada" means “to scrape by” or “barely make a living.” Here, “ipp” refers to the mouth, and “pul” to glue. In this video, the artist visualizes the idiom literally, showing a person actually putting glue in their mouth.
