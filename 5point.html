인생의 오점

2025
종이에 돋보기
18 × 25 × 23 cm
이 작은 다섯 개의 점들이 보이는가? 인생의 오점이란 다시 보면 이렇게 작은 것들이다.
When you look closely through the magnifying lens, you can see five small dots. The Korean word "ojeom" means “a blemish.” However, when broken down, "o" means “five” and "jeom" means “dot.” In this work, the artist visualizes ojeom as five tiny points, suggesting that what once felt like major blemishes in life may ultimately be nothing more than five small dots.
