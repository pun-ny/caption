자, 사진 찍습니다! 하나 둘 셋 찰칵! (퍽)
In Korea, the word jjikda can mean both “to take a photograph” and “to strike or chop something.” The artist draws on this dual meaning, treating the act of chopping a photo with an axe as an act of photographing. This is why the work is titled Camera.
